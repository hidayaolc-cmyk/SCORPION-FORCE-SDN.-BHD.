ABOUT US

Scorpion Force Sdn. Bhd. is a professional security services company established to support national development and increase Bumiputera participation in the security industry. The company was founded based on extensive experience in security operations at both national and international levels.

Our management team consists of former military officers with strong backgrounds in security control and discipline. Their leadership and operational expertise ensure reliable protection for people, property, and assets.

We are committed to delivering trusted, efficient, and robust security services to meet the growing demand for safety across commercial, industrial, and public sectors.

Our Mission

  • To deliver professional security management and safety services
  • To supply reliable security, safety, and industrial protection equipment
  • To provide safety and security training to develop skilled personnel
  • To support human resource development in the security sector
  • To contribute to a safer working environment and national security

Our Vision

To provide quality and excellent service to our customers to become a well-known and best security company in the country

OUR SECURITY SERVICES

Internal Static Guards

Unarmed Security Guards services for High-Risk Buildings, Factories, Condominiums, and Construction Sites.

Armed Guards

Professionally trained Ex-Police and Ex-Army personnel prepared to serve Banks, Goldsmith Outlets, and Warehouses.
